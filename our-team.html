Areas of Practice & Legal Services

Constitutional Law

Writ Petitions under Article 199, constitutional remedies, stay orders, judicial review and public law disputes.

Civil & Property

Property disputes, title claims, civil suits for declaration, possession, partition, injunction, recovery and contractual disputes.

Criminal Litigation

Criminal defense, bail matters, FIR quashment, appeals, revisions, white-collar crimes, NAB and FIA representation.

Family Laws

Divorce, Khula, maintenance, child custody, guardianship certificates, succession, inheritance and family settlements.

Rent & Tenancy

Eviction proceedings, Rent Controller matters, rent disputes, agreement drafting, recovery of arrears and mesne profits.

Revenue & Land

Mutation disputes, land record corrections, partition proceedings, appeals before AC/DC/Commissioner/Board.

Service & Employment

Service Tribunal cases, government employee disputes, departmental inquiries, promotions, termination and reinstatement.

Commercial & Corporate

Commercial disputes, business recovery suits, cheque dishonour, partnership disputes, arbitration and settlements.

Taxation & FBR

FBR notices, income tax and sales tax disputes, appeals before Commissioner (Appeals) and Appellate Tribunal.

Intellectual Property

Trademark and copyright registration, IP infringement litigation, brand protection and enforcement actions.

Registrations, Filings & Documentation

Company & Business

SECP Company Incorporation, Partnership office Registration, NTN/Sales Tax Registration, corporate filings.

NGO / Trust / Society

NGO Registration, Trust Deed drafting, Society registration, NPO documentation and compliance matters.

Real Estate & Housing

Housing society documentation, property transfer verification, sale/gift/lease deeds, CDA/RDA matters.

Trade & Commerce

Chamber of Commerce registration, trade licensing, Export/Import documentation and commercial compliance.
