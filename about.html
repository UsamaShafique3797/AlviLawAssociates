Our Foundation

Upholding the Rule of Law in Pakistan

Alvi Law Associates is a distinguished law firm registered with the Punjab/Islamabad Bar Council, practicing before all courts in Pakistan including the Supreme Court under Article 184(3) and Lahore High Court under Article 199 of the Constitution.

Our advocates specialize in matters governed by the Pakistan Penal Code 1860, Code of Criminal Procedure 1898, Code of Civil Procedure 1908, and all federal and provincial statutes including the Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act (PECA) 2016.

With chambers in Rawalpindi and appearance rights across Punjab, we represent clients before District & Sessions Courts, Banking Courts, Family Courts, Labour Courts, and all superior judiciary forums including the Federal Shariat Court.
