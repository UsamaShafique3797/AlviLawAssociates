01 — Civil Litigation

Civil Litigation

Our firm handles civil matters under the Code of Civil Procedure 1908, including property disputes, suits for declaration and injunction, specific performance, recovery of damages, and partition suits before Civil Courts and District Courts throughout Punjab/Islamabad.

We represent clients in complex civil litigation involving contract disputes, easement rights, pre-emption suits, and declaratory relief before all tiers of the civil judiciary.

Our advocates are experienced in filing and defending suits, applications for temporary injunctions, and executing decrees through proper legal channels.

02 — Criminal Law

Criminal Law Litigation

We provide robust criminal defense under the Pakistan Penal Code 1860 and Code of Criminal Procedure 1898, representing clients in FIR quashing, bail applications, trial defense, and appeals before Sessions Courts and High Courts.

Our advocates appear before Judicial Magistrates, Sessions Judges, Additional Sessions Judges, and the Lahore High Court for criminal appeals and constitutional petitions under Article 199.

We handle cases involving offenses against person and property, white-collar crimes, NAB references, FIA investigations, and Anti-Terrorism Court matters with strategic precision.
