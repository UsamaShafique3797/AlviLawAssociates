01 — Civil & Criminal

Civil & Criminal Litigation

balance Civil Litigation under CPC 1908

Our firm handles civil matters under the Code of Civil Procedure 1908, including property disputes, suits for declaration and injunction, specific performance, recovery of damages, and partition suits before Civil Courts and District Courts throughout Punjab.

gavel Criminal Defense under PPC & CrPC

We provide robust criminal defense under the Pakistan Penal Code 1860 and Code of Criminal Procedure 1898, representing clients in FIR quashing, bail applications (pre-arrest and post-arrest), trial defense, and appeals before Sessions Courts and High Courts.

Our advocates appear before Judicial Magistrates, Sessions Judges, Additional Sessions Judges, and the Lahore High Court for criminal appeals and constitutional petitions under Article 199.

02 — Corporate

Corporate Laws & Commercial Practice

Our corporate law practice provides comprehensive legal support under the Companies Act 2017 and SECP regulations for businesses at every stage — from incorporation and regulatory filings through to compliance, mergers, and winding up proceedings.

We advise on company formation, board governance, shareholder agreements, and regulatory compliance with the Securities and Exchange Commission of Pakistan (SECP), bringing commercial awareness aligned with Pakistani corporate law.

05 — Cyber & Digital

Cyber Crimes & Digital Assets

Digital crimes require specialized defense under the Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act (PECA) 2016. Our cyber law team handles FIA investigations, cybercrime defense, online defamation cases, and digital asset disputes before designated Cyber Courts.

PECA Defense

Defense against FIA investigations, unauthorized access charges, online harassment under Section 21 PECA, and identity theft cases.

Online Defamation

Defamation suits under Section 20 PECA, social media harassment, and cyber stalking complaints filed with FIA Cyber Crime Wing.

Digital Fraud & Financial Crimes

Online banking fraud, cryptocurrency disputes, phishing cases, and electronic evidence handling.

06 — Family & Personal Status

Family & Personal Status Law

Our family law division handles all matters under the Muslim Family Laws Ordinance 1961, Family Courts Act 1964, and Guardian and Wards Act 1890. We provide compassionate yet firm representation before Family Courts throughout Punjab.

Divorce & Khula

Khula petitions, Talaq proceedings, Mubarat, dissolution of marriage under MFLO 1961, and Nikah Nama disputes.

Inheritance & Succession

Succession certificates, inheritance disputes under Islamic law, Wills, and estate administration before Civil Courts.

Custody & Guardianship

Child custody (Hizanat), guardianship petitions under Guardian and Wards Act 1890, and maintenance (Nafaqah) suits.

07 — Constitutional Law

Constitutional Law

Our senior partners are seasoned practitioners in constitutional advocacy before the Lahore High Court under Article 199 and the Supreme Court of Pakistan under Article 184(3) for matters of fundamental rights and public importance.

Constitutional petitions require the highest caliber of legal expertise. Waqas Ahmed Awan, Advocate High Court, personally oversees all writ petitions including Habeas Corpus, Mandamus, Certiorari, Prohibition, and Quo Warranto.
